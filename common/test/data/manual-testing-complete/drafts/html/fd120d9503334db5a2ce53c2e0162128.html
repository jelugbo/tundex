What is edX?
An organization established by MIT and Harvard University that will develop an open-source technology platform to deliver online courses. HEBS will support Harvard and MIT faculty in conducting research on teaching and learning on campus through tools that enrich classroom and laboratory experiences. At the same time, HEBS will also reach learners around the world through online course materials. The HEBS website will begin by hosting MITx and Harvardx content, with the goal of adding content from other universities interested in joining the platform. HEBS will also support the Harvard and MIT faculty in conducting research on teaching and learning.
